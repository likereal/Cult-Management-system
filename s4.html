Vayu which means wind in the Vedas is a sect that represent the Power of music that can help the individual with not only relating to it but also help the person feel various kinds of emotions. The sect teaches the importance of music in their life and how it can play a vital role of all round development of the individual. Music can be a safe space, inspire, bring out various emotions and make them powerful

The Teachings

Music can come in your in various forms, it not only comes from a favourite song but also from nature where the birds chip, rattling of the leaves, sounds of animals, etc. Music can be also help a person through his life and make him feel emotions that they never knew existed. There are different forms of music varying from metal to rock to experimental to industrial to just noise. The sect helps you find the music you relate to and act as a therapy sessions and with the influence of substance it can make the experience extravagant or horrible. The substance used will be marijuana and there will be a guide to help you through the experience the power of music where they will make you every kind emotions. But do not worry as the experience will not be made horrible rather a experience that will help you in the journey to enlightenment.
