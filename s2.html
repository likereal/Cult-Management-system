Bhumi

Intro


*Lord Marshall Applewhite*
*1931-1997*

Bhumi which means earth in the vedas is a sect that represent the trauma that humans hold inside them from their past and fear that they have over many things that can cause them to be afraid of life and people around them. This sect tries to help the members overcome their trauma and fears through rehablitation. We teach the members to accept the things that happened as it made them who they are and not forget it or hide it deep inside them. Your past life is like soil it is up to you whether to let the soil go barren or plant exotic plants and add organic compost and to make it beautiful. It is your job to take care of them , yes we can help but you are the most responsible says

The Teachings

The World is comprised of many problems and each individual is suffering from something or someone and we have to realize that having problems is the only way that a person can enjoy his life. An individual cannot always be happy but that does not mean he has to always be sad. Some people can have a good past and some don't but that does not mean your future needs to be tarnished because of it. Life is all about ups and downs,The main key to all is
1. Acceptance
2. Finding your callings
3. Do not make other's problems your own
4. If you feel lost, we are always ready to help
5. Do not fear as you are always right
6. Others do not matter
7. If you do not want them in your life just remove them from the picture
8. There is no restriction just do it
Do not fear as your actions will be justified in our eyes and remember there is no mistakes just happy accidents

Sow your seed of sorrow and reap a beautiful harvest of happiness
